OUR MISSION

To connect the World’s talent to their next opportunity

Ruben, Artur, and Timur started Career Karma in 2018 after experiencing first hand the struggles of switching into the technology industry from non-traditional backgrounds. They recognized that many in-demand jobs require hands-on experience. The three friends resolved to use peer mentorship to empower others pursuing new careers through technical bootcamps.

In the last decade, bootcamps have proven that most employers value specialized skills. As technology continues to disrupt traditional industries, workers are choosing rapid reskilling programs to increase their earning potential and career security.

With this in mind, Career Karma’s vision is to help one billion people over the next 10 years and to connect the world’s talent to their next opportunities.

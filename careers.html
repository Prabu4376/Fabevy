Welcome to Fabevy

At Fabevy, We believe in creating a work place that is conducive to creativity and productivity. We offers a collaborative, team-oriented, dynamic environment that encourages transparency, mutual respect and continuous learning. We have high energy and passion for what we do! We are always on the lookout for talented individuals who can contribute positively to our collaborative and creative environment. If you are passionate and looking for a company where you can build a career and be happy doing it, Fabevy is the place for you.

Flexibility

We value Flexibility and are proud to offer a variety of ways for our employees to create work=life balance. We offer flexible work hours, telecommuting and compressed work weeks. We understand that life doesn’t always fit into a traditional work schedule, and we are committed to accommodating the needs of our employees.

Advocate Personal Goals

As a company, we advocate that our employees have clear and attainable personal goals for their career development. We believe that this helps our employees stay focused and motivated , and also allows us to better support their career growth. We encourage our employees to regularly review and update their goals.

Commuting and Transit

Commuting can be a time-consuming and stressful part of the workday, but it doesn’t have to be. At Fabevy, we offer a number of options to make your commute easier and more efficient. We offer flexible work hours and the ability to work from home. This way, you can avoid rush hour traffic and save on gas and other commuting costs.

Work life Balance

Work-life balance is something important to us [Fabevy]. We understand that our employees have lives outside of work and that they need time to focus on other things. We offer flexible work hours, remote work options and also telecommuting to make sure that our employees are able to manage their work and their personal lives.

Continuous Learning

As a growing company, we firmly believe in continuous learning. We know that a commitment to continuous learning can help our employees reach their full potential and build rewarding careers with our company. We’re proud to invest in our employees’ development, and we hope that our career page will inspire you to start learning today!

Leadership Mentoring

We believe that a mentoring program can be a great way to help our employees to develop their leadership skills. Through active 1-on-1 conversations, mentees uncover areas where they need to improve their skills. By pairing up like this, we can create a supportive environment for learning and growth.

Know about Fabevy

Fabevy provides affordable and efficient training to active jobseekers & graduates who are passionate about starting their IT career. We transform jobseekers into professionals by upskilling them.

We Stand Out because

We successfully provide students with intensive courses by India's top faculties and personal mentors. Fabevy strives to make the learning experience comprehensive and accessible for job-seekers. We believe in empowering every graduate who struggle to kick-start his/her career.

Our Key Focus Areas

Fabevy's main focus is to make skill-development program as economical as possible for all first-gen graduates. With our affordable & placement guranteed courses we have been able to provide a platform for 100s of IT career aspirants.

What Makes Us Different

Fabevy strives to focus on Placement-First programs which would ensure every trainee to land in a job after the successful completion of our progam. Industry relevant curriculum and portfolio building based learning experience differentiate Fabevy from tradition training academies.

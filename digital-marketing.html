Digital Marketing Course Details

Become a certified digital marketer, perfect your online marketing skills, develop a strategy, learn tools,techniques, nalytics and accelerate your career.

LEARNING PATH

Learn Fundamentals of Digital Marketing

Begin with understanding the basic concepts, terminology, and channels of digital marketing. Get an overview of SEO, social media marketing, email marketing, and PPC advertising.

Phase-1: Creating and Executing a Strategy

Learn how to create a digital marketing strategy, set objectives, and define target audiences. Understand how to research your competition, analyze your audience, and develop a marketing plan.

Phase-2: Marketing Tools and Platforms

Discover the different marketing tools and platforms available, such as Google Analytics, AdWords, Facebook Ads, and email marketing software. Learn how to set up and use these tools effectively.

Phase-3: Data Analytics and CRO

Gain an understanding of how to measure and analyze data to optimize your digital marketing efforts. Explore conversion rate optimization (CRO) techniques to improve the performance of your campaigns.

Phase-4: Content Marketing

Learn to develop a content strategy, and create different types of content, such as blog posts, videos, and infographics. Use content to drive traffic, engagement, and conversions.

Phase 5: Run a Campaign Plan

Run a campaign plan on your own using various tools and techniques and different elements of digital marketing and check the campaign effectiveness using analytical tools.
